"Here's My Speech"

Good day, everyone. I am Mark Christian S. Rivera, and today I want to speak about the issue of discrimination. Discrimination is a force that prevents progress and creates division within society. It happens when people are treated unfairly because of their race, gender, religion, or disabilityâ€”things that should never define a person's worth or limit their opportunities.

A great example of standing up against this injustice is Pedro Pelaez. With his deep knowledge of law, he boldly spoke out against unfair treatment and didn't hesitate to criticize both Spanish and Filipino priests when necessary. Pelaez argued that these actions contradicted the Christian belief that all people are equal before God.

In 1850, he published El Clero Filipino, a protest against these unjust practices. His actions remind us that discrimination should always be challenged. No one should be treated differently because of their background or beliefs. We must continue to fight for equality and fairness for all.
